Critical Role

The group who brought Dungeons & Dragons into the modern world.

"Hello everyone, and welcome to tonight's episode of Critical Role, where a bunch of us nerdy-ass voice actors sit around and play Dungeons & Dragons!"

- Intro to the videos, done by Matthew Mercer

Below you will find a brief timeline of Critical Role's history

Campaign One (C1)

  • circa 2012 - The group begins their first campaign (with Matthew Mercer as DM) as a private home game. The group name was the Super High-Intensity Team (S.H.I.T.s) prior to streaming.
  • March 2015 - Critical Role begins streaming their first campaign on the Geek & Sundry Twitch and YouTube channels. The group name was changed to Vox Machina. This campaign lasted until October of 2017 with a total of 115 episodes.
    • Characters from C1
      • Grog Strongjaw (Travis Willingham): Goliath Barbarian (Path of the Berserker)/Fighter (Battle Master)
      • Pike Trickfoot (Ashley Johnson): Gnome Cleric (War Domain)
      • Vex'ahlia de Rolo (Laura Bailey): Half-elf Ranger (Beast Master)/Rogue (Inquisitive)
      • Vax'ildan (Liam O'Brien): Half-elf Rogue (Assassin)/Paladin (Oath of Vengeance)
      • Keyleth (Marisha Ray): Half-elf Druid (Circle of the Moon)
      • Percival de Rolo (Taliesin Jaffe): Human Fighter (Gunslinger)
      • Scanlan Shorthalt (Sam Riegel): Forest Gnome Bard (College of Lore)
      • Tiberius Stormwind: Dragonborn Sorcerer (Draconic Bloodline)
        • Tiberius left Vox Machina after episode 27.
        • Tiberius was played by Orion Acaba until he left the group, as announced on October 28th, 2015.

Campaign Two (C2)

  • January 2018 - Critical Role begins streaming their second campaign with Matthew Mercer as the DM again. They continued to stream on the Geek & Sundry Twitch and YouTube Channels until February 2019.
  • February 2019 - Critical Role ends their partnership with Geek & Sundry and begins their own Twitch and YouTube channels, where they continue to stream and produce content.
  • March 2020 - Critical Role suspends live broadcasts of C2 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. They released pre-recorded content (following COVID-19 safety guidelines) on their YouTube channel.
  • July 2020 - Critical Role resumes releasing pre-recorded episodes for C2. This campaign aired for 141 episodes and ended in June 2021.
    • Characters from C2
      • Fjord (Travis Willingham): Half-orc Warlock(The Hexblade, Pact of the Blade)/Paladin(Oath of the Open Sea)
      • Yasha Nydoorin (Ashley Johnson): Aasimar Barbarian (Path of the Zealot)
      • Jester Lavorre (Laura Bailey): Tiefling Cleric (Trickery Domain)
      • Caleb Widogast (Liam O'Brein): Human Wizard (School of Transmutation)
      • Beauregard "Beau" Lionett (Marisha Ray): Human Monk (Way of the Cobalt Soul)
      • Mollymauk "Molly" Tealeaf (Taliesin Jaffe): Tiefling Blood Hunter (Order of the Ghostslayer)
      • Caduceus Clay (Taliesin Jaffe): Firbolg Cleric (Grave Domain)
      • Nott the Brave (Sam Riegel): Goblin Rogue (Arcane Trickster)/Wizard
        • Nott later became known as Veth Brenatto, who is a Lightfoot Halfling
