Critical Role

The group who brought Dungeons & Dragons into the modern world.

"Hello everyone, and welcome to tonight's episode of Critical Role, where a bunch of us nerdy-ass voice actors sit around and play Dungeons & Dragons!"

Below you will find a brief timeline of Critical Role's history

  • circa 2012 - The group begins their first campaign (with Matthew Mercer as DM) as a private home game. The group name was the Super High-Intensity Team (S.H.I.T.s) prior to streaming.
  • March 2015 - Critical Role begins streaming their first campaign on the Geek & Sundry Twitch and YouTube channels. This campaign lasted until October of 2017 with a total of 115 episodes.
    • Characters from C1
      • Grog Strongjaw (Travis Willingham): Goliath Barbarian (Path of the Berserker)/Fighter (Battle Master)
      • Pike Trickfoot (Ashley Johnson): Gnome Cleric (War Domain)
      • Vex'ahlia de Rolo (Laura Bailey): Half-elf Ranger (Beast Master)/Rogue (Inquisitive)
      • Vax'ildan (Liam O'Brien): Half-elf Rogue (Assassin)/Paladin (Oath of Vengeance)
      • Keyleth (Marisha Ray): Half-elf Druid (Circle of the Moon)
      • Percival de Rolo (Taiesin Jaffe): Human Fighter (Gunslinger)
      • Scanlan Shorthalt (Sam Riegel): Forest Gnome Bard (College of Lore)
      • Tiberius Stormwind: Dragonborn Sorcerer (Draconic Bloodline)
        • Tiberius was played by Orion Acaba until he left the group, as announced on October 28th, 2015.
